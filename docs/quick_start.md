# Quick Start Guide: From Zero to First Message

Follow this checklist to get your WhatsApp Business Platform up and running in under 10 minutes.

### Step 1: Account Setup
- [ ] Log in to the application.
- [ ] Navigate to **System > Connect Account**.
- [ ] Click **Connect with Facebook** and complete the OAuth flow.
- [ ] confirm that your "Quality Rating" and "Messaging Limit" are visible.

### Step 2: Add a Test Contact
- [ ] Go to **Subscriber Manager**.
- [ ] Click **Add Contact**.
- [ ] Enter your own phone number (with country code).
- [ ] Save the contact.

### Step 3: Send a Message
- [ ] Go to **Shared Inbox**.
- [ ] Search for your phone number.
- [ ] Click on the conversation.
- [ ] Since this is a new conversation, click the **Template** icon (paper/list icon).
- [ ] Select a "hello_world" or similar welcome template.
- [ ] Click **Send**.
- [ ] **Verify**: Check your phone to see if the message arrived!

### Step 4: Build a Simple Bot
- [ ] Go to **Bot Manager**.
- [ ] Click **Create New Automation**.
- [ ] Name it "Test Bot".
- [ ] Set Trigger Keyword to `test`.
- [ ] Add a **Text Node** saying "Hello! I am working."
- [ ] Connect the **Start** node to the **Text Node**.
- [ ] Click **Save**.
- [ ] **Test**: Reply "test" from your phone to your business number.

### Step 5: Setup Billing
- [ ] Go to **Billing**.
- [ ] Check your default **Plan**.
- [ ] **Top up** your wallet (if in production) or verify the demo balance.

### Step 6: Configure Commerce
- [ ] Go to **Commerce > Settings**.
- [ ] Enable the commerce engine.
- [ ] Add your first product in **Product Manager**.
- [ ] **Verify**: Send a product catalog message to your test contact from the CRM/Inbox.

### Done!
You are now ready to explore advanced features like Campaigns, AI Brain, and CRM integration. Refer to the [User Guide](user_guide.md) for more details.
